# Shramic Networks - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. What is Shramic Networks?

Shramic Networks is an AI-powered infrastructure platform that connects workers, employers, and communities through intelligent job matching, fair wage intelligence, and trusted workforce solutions.

## 2. Who founded Shramic Networks?

Shramic Networks was founded by Mutturaj Goudra, who serves as the Founder and CEO.

## 3. When was Shramic Networks founded?

Shramic Networks was founded in March 2025.

## 4. What is the mission of Shramic Networks?

The mission is to create employment opportunities, improve rural livelihoods, provide fair wages, and connect workers, farmers, experts, and businesses through digital infrastructure.

## 5. What is the vision of Shramic Networks?

The vision is to build a national rural employment and services network powered by technology, data intelligence, and transparent pricing systems.

## 6. Where is Shramic Networks located?

The registered office is located in Hanamsagar, Koppal, Karnataka, India.

## 7. What industries does Shramic Networks serve?

Shramic Networks serves agriculture, construction, livestock, rural businesses, and rural services.

## 8. What makes Shramic Networks different?

Shramic combines AI-powered matching, fair wage intelligence, and a physical trust network called Mitra Network.

## 9. What problem does Shramic Networks solve?

It solves workforce shortages, unreliable labour discovery, wage transparency issues, and dependence on middlemen.

## 10. How does Shramic Networks support rural communities?

It creates job opportunities, promotes fair wages, and helps workers connect directly with employers.

## 11. What is the AI Job Matching Engine?

It is a smart system that matches workers to jobs based on skills, location, availability, and demand.

## 12. How does AI matching work?

The system analyzes worker profiles and employer requirements to find the best matches.

## 13. What factors are considered during matching?

Skills, location, availability, work history, job requirements, and demand patterns.

## 14. How does location-based matching work?

The system prioritizes jobs and workers within nearby geographical regions.

## 15. How does availability affect matching?

Workers who are available for specific dates and times are matched first.

## 16. What is the AI Fair Pricing Engine?

It is an AI-powered wage benchmarking system that recommends fair wages.

## 17. How are wage benchmarks calculated?

The engine analyzes demand, supply, skill level, location, experience, seasonality, and market conditions.

## 18. How many job roles are supported by the pricing engine?

The system supports over 200 job roles.

## 19. How does fair pricing benefit workers?

It helps workers receive transparent and fair wages.

## 20. How does fair pricing benefit employers?

It provides reliable wage benchmarks and reduces disputes.

## 21. What is Shramic Krushi?

Shramic Krushi is a workforce and machinery management platform for agriculture.

## 22. Who can use Shramic Krushi?

Farmers, agribusinesses, cooperatives, and agriculture service providers.

## 23. Does Krushi support machinery management?

Yes, it helps coordinate machinery access and utilization.

## 24. Can farmers hire workers through Krushi?

Yes, farmers can discover and hire suitable workers.

## 25. What are the benefits of Krushi?

Workforce planning, machinery coordination, productivity tracking, and expert support.
